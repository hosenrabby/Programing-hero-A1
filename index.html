Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is fostering innovation through technology,startups,and sustainable solutions,especially in agriculture,fintech,and digital services.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is advancing sustainability through renewable energy,eco-friendly practices,waste management,and climate-resilient agricultural initiatives.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is rapidly developing infrastructure,focusing on transportation, urbanization,energy,and digital connectivity to support economic growth.

Empowerment

Bangladesh is promoting empowerment through education, gender equality, skill development, and economic opportunities, fostering inclusive social progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh, a land of vibrant culture and resilience, strives for a future where prosperity and innovation flourish. With a rich history and unwavering spirit, it seeks to empower every citizen through education, technology, and sustainable growth. The nation envisions a harmonious society, fostering unity, diversity, and global collaboration. As it marches towards progress, Bangladesh aims to be a beacon of hope, opportunity, and excellence on the world stage.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
